copyrighted artists

a fun game of drawing and guessing!

  • players are split into groups of two
  • one player from each pair is assigned a prompt to draw
  • their partner has to try and copy their drawing
  • after everyone is done, the drawings are revealed in their pairs and everyone else votes for which one they think is the original
  • points are awarded for guessing correctly and for receiving votes
